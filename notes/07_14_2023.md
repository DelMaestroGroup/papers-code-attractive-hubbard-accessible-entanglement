## Shannon entropies
Regular Shannon entropy:

$$
H_1 = -\sum_n P_n \log P_n
$$

![Alt text](../plots/07_14_2023/Shannon_ent_1.svg)

Renyi-generalized Shannon entropy:

$$
H_{1/2} = -2\sum_n \log (P_n)^{1/2}
$$

![Alt text](../plots/07_14_2023/Shannon_ent_2.svg)
